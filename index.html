Welcome to Firux Linux

Firux Linux is a cutting-edge operating system based on Arch Linux, designed to provide a modern and customizable experience for developers and users. Our distribution offers a range of features aimed at enhancing productivity and simplifying system management.

Why Choose Firux Linux?

  • Based on Arch Linux: Enjoy the power and flexibility of Arch Linux with additional enhancements tailored to your needs.
  • Customizable Desktop Environment: Personalize your desktop with a variety of themes and settings to match your workflow.
  • Simplified Installer with Pakman: Install Firux Linux quickly and easily using our streamlined Pakman installer.
